Check if you are eligible for the Warm Home Discount Scheme in England and Wales

You do not need to apply for the Warm Home Discount. You’ll be paid automatically by your energy supplier.

Use this service to find out:

  • if you’re eligible for the scheme in England and Wales
  • what to do if you did not get a letter about the Warm Home Discount but think you may be eligible
If you live in a park home, you will need to apply for the Park Homes Warm Home Discount Scheme instead.
Start now

Before you start

You'll be asked about:

  • the name of your electricity supplier
  • details of any benefits you receive
  • the type, age and size of your property

If your property has an energy performance certificate (EPC), you can use your postcode to fill in your property details automatically.

If your property doesn’t have an EPC, you’ll need to manually enter these details. However, we won’t be able give you a definite answer. Instead, we’ll estimate your eligibility and tell you what to do next.
