Check if you are eligible for the Warm Home Discount Scheme (England and Wales)

Most households eligible for the Warm Home Discount Scheme will automatically receive the discount from their electricity supplier.

If you are eligible, you will receive a notice letter from the government before the middle of January 2023.

Use this service to:

  • check if you might be eligible for the Warm Home Discount Scheme
  • learn what to do if you believe you might be eligible for the discount, but have not been contacted by the government

If you live in a park home, you will need to apply for the Park Homes Warm Home Discount Scheme instead.

Start now

What you need

You'll be asked about:

  • your electricity supplier
  • any benefits you receive
  • your property

You'll need to know the size and age of your property. This information can be found:

  • on an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)
  • on the Energy Performance of Buildings Register (opens in new tab)
  • on documentation such as your tenancy agreement or insurance paperwork
